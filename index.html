A Typical Weekday!

About this

This is a typical, mundane work week in the life of James Thompson! My commutes to work start in Mount Sterling, where I live, and end at Palmer Engineering Company, where I am a GIS Analyst. After work, if I do not return home immediately, I enjoy going to Legacy Grove Park for a walk/run, teaching judo at Fifth Element BJJ, or stuffing my face with the best pizza ever from La Trattoria. The last of these I have enjoyed a little too often lately!
